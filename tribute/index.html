Robert Downey Jr.

The Phoenix of Hollywood — A legacy of resilience, redemption, and cinematic genius

The Chapters of a Legendary Comeback

1992

The Chaplin Masterclass

Earns his first Academy Award nomination for Best Actor for his brilliant portrayal of Charlie Chaplin, proving his immense technical acting depth early on.

2003

The Turning Point

After a tumultuous period that nearly destroyed his career, he makes a definitive commitment to sobriety and begins working his way back up through independent film roles.

2008

I Am Iron Man

Marvel Studios takes a legendary gamble and casts him as Tony Stark. Iron Man becomes a massive global hit, launching the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) and cementing RDJ as a global superstar.

2024

Academy Award Glory

Wins his first Oscar for Best Supporting Actor for his incredible, transformative dramatic performance as Lewis Strauss in Christopher Nolan's Oppenheimer.
